Pixelate

GDES Multi Studio 1 - Spring 2026

Course Facilitators: Ayza Sheikh / Syed Erzum Naqvi / Fatima Abbas

A collection of video game skins for arcade classics "Pacman" and "Space Invaders" produced by students of Graphic Design at VCUarts Qatar. This project explores worldbuilding and system design, allowing students to explore modular narratives within preset structures. Each student has developed every individual asset for the game themselves, from backgrounds and characters to the various HUD elements, exploring how a collection of unique elements can work together to tell a more comprehensive story. Their work ranges from hyperlocal depictions of Qatari culture to fantastical renditions of space, aliens, and magic.
